Flan

Description

A classic Mexican dessert of custard with soft caramel topping

Ingredients

    For the caramel

  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water

    • For the flan

  • 1 can (14 oz / 397 g) lechera
  • 1 can (12 oz / 354 ml) evaporated milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Make the caramel

    • In a sucepan over medium heat, add the sugar and water.
    • Let it melt without stirring until it turns a golden amber color.
    • Carefully pour the caramel into a flan mold or baking dish.
    • Tilt the mold to coat the bottom evenly. Set aside.

  2. Prepare teh flan mixture

    • In a blender, add:
      • Condensed milk
      • Evaporated milk
      • Eggs
      • Vanilla
    • Blend until smooth (about 20-30 seconds).

  3. Assemble

    • Pour the flan mixture over the hardened caramel in the mold.

  4. Bake in a water bath (baño María)

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Place the flan mold inside a larger baking dish.
    • Add hot water to the larger dish until it reaches halfway up the flan mold.
    • Cover the flan loosely with foil.

  5. Bake

    • Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until center is set but slightly jiggly.

  6. Cool and chill

    • Remove from the oven and water bath.
    • Let it cool to room temperature.
    • Refrigerate for at least 4 hours (overnight is best).

  7. Unmold & serve

    • Run a knife around the edges.
    • Invert onto a plate and let the caramel flow over the flan.
